Why you need to make Pikliz

Pikliz is a spicy Haitian pickled slaw made with cabbage, carrots, onions, and Scotch bonnet peppers. Its tangy, fiery crunch pairs perfectly with fried foods, grilled meats, and rice dishes, adding a burst of flavor to every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 2 red or yellow bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 medium red onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 Jamaican Scotch bonnet pepper
  • 3 tablespoons whole allspice
  • 4 peppercorns
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 cups white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
