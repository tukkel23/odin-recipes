Why you need to make Banh Mi

Banh Mi is a Vietnamese sandwich on a crisp baguette. Filled with savory meat (like grilled pork or pâté), pickled veggies, fresh cilantro, cucumber, and chilies. Topped with mayo and Maggi seasoning. A perfect fusion of French and Vietnamese flavors.

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Grilled pork/paneer
  • Carrots (julienned)
  • Daikon radish (julienned)
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar and Salt
  • Cucumber
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Mayonnaise
  • Maggi seasoning or soy sauce

Directions/Steps

  1. Make quick-pickled veggies: Julienne carrots and daikon. Soak in vinegar, sugar, salt, and water for at least 30 mins.
  2. Prepare protein: Grill, cook, or slice chosen meat/tofu.
  3. Slice baguette horizontally, leaving one edge attached.
  4. Lightly toast or warm the baguette.
  5. Spread mayonnaise generously on both inner sides of the bread.
  6. Layer the bottom half with cooked protein.
  7. Top protein with drained pickled veggies, cucumber slices, cilantro, and chilies.
  8. Drizzle with Maggi/soy sauce (and Sriracha if desired). Close sandwich and serve.

Optional: Spread pâté inside the bread before mayo in step 5, if using.
